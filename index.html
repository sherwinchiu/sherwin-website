About

I studied Computer Engineering at the University of Waterloo, with coursework in distributed computing, real-time operating systems, compilers, and concurrency. I’m now a full-time Software Engineer at Geotab on the OEM team, building cloud integrations that connect manufacturer vehicle APIs into MyGeotab. Before that, my co-ops took me through Geotab, Motive, and Swift Medical — touching cloud infra, mobile, and medical imaging.

On the side I founded Electrium Mobility, a UWaterloo design team that builds custom electric vehicles, and I keep a steady stream of personal hardware projects going — custom ESP32 boards, force-feedback VR gloves, sound-reactive sculptures, the works.
